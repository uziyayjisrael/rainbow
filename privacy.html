Bow Platform Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 23, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to the Bow platform. The Bow platform is developed, maintained, and held by Masokoane (the "Developer"), and operated in partnership with the Rainbow Meter Taxi Association. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy informs you how Masokoane and the Association handle your personal data when you use our application and tells you about your privacy rights.

Information We Collect

We collect and process various types of personal data to provide and improve our services:

  • Identity Data: Name, username, and driver's license information.
  • Contact Data: Phone number and email address.
  • Location Data: Real-time location to facilitate ride requests and navigation.
  • Payment Data: Payment card details and transaction history (processed securely via Paystack).
  • Device Data: IP address, device type, and app usage statistics.

How We Use Your Data

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate ride bookings and transportation services.
  • To process payments and prevent fraud.
  • To provide customer support and safety features.
  • To improve our app performance and user experience.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

Data Sharing

We share data with:

  • Masokoane: As the platform holder and developer, Masokoane has access to system data to ensure platform stability, security, and continuous improvement.
  • Drivers/Passengers: Necessary information to facilitate the ride.
  • Service Providers: Payment processors (Paystack), cloud hosting, and analytics tools.
  • Law Enforcement: When required by law or to protect user safety.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can manage most of your information through the app settings or contact us directly for specific requests.
