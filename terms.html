Association Standards & Conduct

Drivers operating on the Bow platform are members of the Rainbow Meter Taxi Association and are bound by the Association's Code of Conduct. This includes:

  • Adhering to professional behavior and non-discriminatory practices.
  • Strict adherence to local municipal bylaws and transportation regulations.

Fare Regulation

Fares are calculated based on the Rainbow Meter Taxi Association approved rates. While the Bow platform provide estimates and digital metering, the final fare is governed by Association policy. Any discrepancies or fare disputes should be reported through the app for Association review.

Payments and Fees

Payments for rides are handled securely through the app using Paystack. The Bow platform is committed to total transparency regarding our fee structure:

  • Platform Commission: 0% (Rainbow Association Policy).
  • Driver Earnings: 100% of the quoted fare goes to the driver and vehicle owner.
  • Third-Party Processing: Standard Paystack transaction fees (e.g., 2.9% + R1.00 for card payments) are deducted from the payout to cover payment gateway costs.
  • Waiting Charges: R0.20 per minute (applied after an initial grace period).
  • Cancellation Fee: A flat R5.00 fee may apply if a ride is cancelled more than 2 minutes after a driver has accepted the request.

All estimates and potential surcharges are displayed in the app before you confirm your ride.

Safety & Emergency Features

In accordance with South African safety regulations, the Bow platform includes a mandatory Emergency SOS (Panic Button). Users can trigger an emergency alert by shaking their mobile device during an active ride, which immediately notifies Association dispatch and security responders with your real-time location.
